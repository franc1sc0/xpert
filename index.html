Welcome to XPERT's Homepage.

XPERT is a tool for identifying cross-browser issues in web applications. The public release is a re-implementation of the tool described in the X-PERT paper, to meet the terms of licensing and contract from Fujitsu labs.

For updates on the development of this project, please watch this page or email @shauvik to be added to a notification mailing list.
